FAQ

Common questions.

Will families know it's AI?
Most can't tell. The system responds in under a second with natural, empathetic conversation. It handles interruptions, emotional callers, and complex questions with care. If anyone asks to speak with a person, they're offered a callback from your admissions team within 2 hours.
Can it handle sensitive conversations about placing a parent?
Yes. The AI is prompted to be warm, patient, and never rush a caller. It acknowledges the difficulty of the decision and guides the conversation with empathy. It's not trying to close — it's trying to help and book a tour so your admissions team can take over in person.
How long does setup take?
48 hours from kickoff to live calls. We handle everything: writing your intake script, connecting your calendar and CRM, and testing with realistic scenarios before going live.
What happens during the free pilot?
We answer your phones for 2 weeks at no cost. At the end, we show you exactly how many calls were answered, how many tours were booked, and what you would have missed. You decide if it's worth it.
How is this different from A Place for Mom?
A Place for Mom charges 1-2 months of rent per referral and owns the relationship with the family. We answer YOUR phone, book tours on YOUR calendar, and you own every lead. No commission per move-in. Flat monthly rate.
What if we already have admissions staff?
Perfect — we handle overflow and after-hours. When your team is in a meeting, giving a tour, at lunch, or gone for the day, we catch everything they miss. No inquiry falls through the cracks.
